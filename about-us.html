About National Driver Solutions
National Driver Solutions is a corporate business that feels like a family. NDS is a logistics and transportation solutions company. We are committed to consistently providing safe, qualified and customer service-oriented drivers to our clients.
WE DO THE WORK
NDS recruits, screens, hires and deploys the drivers you need for your company or job. If you are a driver, we will match you with the right employer to ensure your success.
WE ARE EFFICIENT
You tell us your company's needs, and we will provide experienced and professional drivers. For drivers looking to join our family, once approved you will be up and running in 5 days or less.
SAFETY IS OUR PRIORITY
Drivers are thoroughly screened to meet DOT requirements, and receive ongoing training on DOT rules and safety.
